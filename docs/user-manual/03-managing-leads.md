# 03 — Managing Leads

How to find, filter, regenerate, and clean up your dossiers.

## The Leads list

Click **Leads** in the top nav. You'll see a table with one row per dossier, ordered newest-first.

> _Screenshot placeholder: `./screenshots/leads-list.png`_

Columns:
- **Lead name** — the prospect's name (or "Unknown" if the dossier couldn't determine one).
- **Company** — the organization.
- **Score** — composite score 0-100 with the tier badge.
- **ICP** — 1-5 stars.
- **Created** — when the dossier was generated.
- **Status** — `Done`, `Partial`, or `Failed`.

Click any row to open its detail page.

## Search

The search box at the top of the list matches against:
- Lead name
- Lead title
- Company name
- Email

Search is case-insensitive and substring-based. Example: typing `mensah` finds "Perry Amo-Mensah" because "mensah" is a substring of the last name.

## Filters

Three filter dropdowns / chip-toggles next to the search:

### Tier filter

Toggle tier badges (HOT / WARM / COOL / COLD). Multi-select — click multiple tiers to see all of them together. Default: all tiers shown.

### ICP rating filter

Click the star icons to show only leads with ICP ≥ N stars. Click again to clear.

### Status filter

Show only `Done`, `Partial`, or `Failed` dossiers. Useful when you want to revisit failed runs and decide whether to retry.

## Sorting

Click any column header to sort:
- **Score** — by composite_score descending (default).
- **Created** — by date.
- **Lead name** / **Company** — alphabetic.

A second click reverses the order.

## Pagination

If you have more than 50 leads, the list paginates. Use the controls at the bottom-right.

## Opening a lead

Clicking a row navigates to **/leads/:leadId**. The detail page has:

- **Header card** — score, tier, key drivers, copy-URL button.
- **Iframe** — the full HTML dossier (Tabs 1 and 2).
- **Regenerate** button (top-right of the header).
- **Delete** button (admin only — see [`05-admin-tasks.md`](./05-admin-tasks.md)).

> _Screenshot placeholder: `./screenshots/lead-detail.png`_

### Iframe behavior

The dossier renders inside a sandboxed iframe — your browser may block certain interactions (right-click "Open in new tab", etc.). The **Copy** buttons inside the dossier *do* work because we set up a `postMessage` bridge.

If you want to open the raw HTML in a new tab (e.g., to print or save), use the **Open in new tab** button next to the iframe.

## Regenerating a lead

For when the lead's situation may have changed — new exec hire, recent breach, fresh budget cycle.

1. Open the lead detail page.
2. Click **Regenerate**.
3. The intake modal opens **pre-filled** with the previous values.
4. Optionally edit them (e.g., add new notes).
5. Submit.

**A new lead row is created.** The old dossier stays at its old URL — frozen. Both appear in your **Leads** list.

This design is intentional: if you've already shared the old dossier with a teammate or customer, regenerating doesn't break that link. If you want the *new* version, share the new URL.

The new lead's `report_date` and `eliss_version` reflect the regeneration, not the original.

## Comparing two versions of the same lead

After regenerating, you can compare:

1. Open both lead detail pages (e.g., in two browser tabs).
2. Switch between them to see what changed (score moved, new signals, updated DMU, etc.).

There's no built-in diff view at v1.0.0 — but the side-by-side tabs work fine.

## Deleting a lead

Lead deletion is **admin-only** — see [`05-admin-tasks.md`](./05-admin-tasks.md).

Why: deletion cascades through `lead_signals` (gone) and deletes the Stratus HTML. If you regret a deletion, the data is unrecoverable. The team chose to gate this behind admin role.

If you have a delete request and you're not admin, ask one. The admin can delete on your behalf.

## What you can't see

Your **Leads** list shows **only your own** dossiers — leads generated by other users (other sales reps, etc.) are not visible to you. The application enforces this server-side; even an admin sees their own dossiers in their list (admins use a separate user-management view to assist other users).

If you and a teammate both generated a dossier for the same prospect, you'll each see your own version. Neither sees the other's.

## Lead lifecycle

Each lead has a lifecycle:

```
Created → Done | Partial | Failed
   ↓
(regenerated)
   ↓
A new lead is created (the original is unchanged)
   ↓
(deleted by admin — irreversible)
```

There's no "archive" or "soft-delete" state. Leads either exist (and show in the list) or don't.
